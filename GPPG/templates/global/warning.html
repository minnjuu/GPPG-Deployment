WARNING: Palawan Pangolin is a Protected Species

The Palawan Pangolin (Manis culionensis) is:

  • Critically Endangered Species
  • Endemic to Palawan
  • Protected under Republic Act No. 9147

Legal Consequences for Hunting/Poaching:

  • Imprisonment of 6-12 years
  • Fines ranging from ₱100,000 to ₱1,000,000
  • Additional penalties for trafficking and possession
  • Immediate confiscation of specimens and equipment
